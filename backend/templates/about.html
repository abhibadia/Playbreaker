About Us

We’re Abhi and Surya, the creators of Playbreaker. Our mission is to empower basketball players at every level with the advanced analysis and insights usually reserved for elite athletes.

Abhi

Abhi is passionate about basketball and technology. With a strong background in sports analytics, he leads the innovation at Playbreaker, ensuring every player has access to professional-grade insights.

Surya

Surya brings a creative approach to data-driven basketball analysis. His expertise blends innovative strategies with advanced analytics to push the boundaries of how players understand and improve their game.

How Playbreaker Was Developed

Playbreaker was born out of a shared passion for basketball and a vision to democratize elite sports analysis. Combining years of experience and dedication, we set out to create a platform that bridges the gap between professional insights and everyday athletes.

Starting from long nights of brainstorming to rigorous testing on the court, every step of our journey has been driven by the desire to empower players at all levels. Our iterative process involved close collaboration with athletes, coaches, and tech experts to fine-tune the features and ensure the platform meets the real-world needs of basketball enthusiasts.

Our Mission

At Playbreaker, our mission is to raise the bar for basketball performance analysis. We believe that every player, regardless of their level, deserves access to cutting-edge technology and insights to enhance their game and reach their full potential.

Through continuous innovation and a deep understanding of the sport, we strive to provide tools that not only analyze performance but also inspire improvement. Our commitment is to build a community where data meets passion, transforming the way basketball is played and enjoyed.
